# DentCraft Competitor Analysis

## Summary

5 main competitors in Satu Mare. All use WordPress. None except Dr. Telegdy have multilingual sites. Sala Diagnostics dominates local search through review volume (88 reviews). Art Dental and Finess Dental rank well through keyword-optimized landing pages.

---

## Competitor 1: Sala Diagnostics (Dr. Sala Tudor)

**Website:** https://www.saladiagnostics.ro/
**Address:** Strada Careiului C20, Satu Mare
**Hours:** Mon-Fri 08:00-16:00
**Google Reviews:** 88 (4.8 stars, 93% are 5-star)
**Tech:** WordPress + Divi + NitroPack CDN
**Languages:** Romanian only

**Services:** Dental radiology (CBCT), implants, prosthetics, cosmetic, endodontics under microscope, laser, surgery, orthodontics, prophylaxis.

**What they do well:**
- Strongest review profile in Satu Mare. This is why they dominate the local pack.
- Blog with locally-targeted SEO articles ("Cum alegi un dentist bun in Satu Mare?")
- Trustindex verification badge
- "Firma de Aur" award winner 2022-2024
- Equipment marketing (microscope, CT scanner, laser, "robots")

**Weaknesses:**
- Closes at 4 PM (limited hours)
- Romanian only
- WordPress/Divi is slower than Next.js

**Threat level:** HIGH — the review volume alone makes them the hardest competitor to overtake in local pack.
