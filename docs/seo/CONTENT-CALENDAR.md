## Content Quality Checklist (Per Post)

Before publishing any blog post:

- [ ] 1,500+ words, unique content (not translated/spun)
- [ ] Primary keyword in title, H1, first 100 words, and 2-3 H2s
- [ ] "Satu Mare" in title for local SEO
- [ ] Meta title under 60 characters with primary keyword
- [ ] Meta description under 155 characters with keyword + CTA
- [ ] At least 2 internal links to service pages
- [ ] At least 1 internal link to another blog post
- [ ] 1 clear CTA (booking, phone 0741 199 977, WhatsApp)
- [ ] FAQ section at end (3-5 questions for FAQ schema)
- [ ] Featured image with descriptive alt text
- [ ] Content answers the search intent honestly
- [ ] At least 3 authenticity signals (see blog-writer skill)
- [ ] Reads naturally — no AI-sounding phrases
- [ ] Translated to EN and HU within 1 week
