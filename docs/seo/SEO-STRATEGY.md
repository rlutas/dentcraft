## Strategy: 5 Priorities

### Priority 1: Get "dentist satu mare" to Page 1
**Current:** Position 10.52 (4,290 impressions, 35 clicks)
**Target:** Position 3-5

**Actions:**
- Optimize homepage title tag: "Dentist Satu Mare | Clinica Stomatologica DentCraft"
- Add "dentist satu mare" to H1 on homepage
- Build 3-5 internal links from service pages pointing to homepage with "dentist satu mare" anchor
- Ensure Google Business Profile name matches
- Get listed on 10+ Romanian dental directories (NAP citations)

### Priority 2: Create Children's Dentistry Content
**Current:** Position 30+ for all "copii" variants (1,851 combined impressions, 0 clicks)
**Target:** Position 1-5

**Actions:**
- Create a dedicated Pedodontie/Stomatologie Copii service page (800+ words)
- Target keywords: "stomatolog copii satu mare", "stomatologie copii satu mare", "dentist copii satu mare"
- Include: age-appropriate procedures, what to expect, when to bring your child
- Add to main navigation under Services
- Translate to EN and HU

### Priority 3: Google Reviews Campaign
**Current:** ~14 reviews
**Target:** 50+ reviews within 6 months, 100+ within 12 months

**Actions:**
- Create a simple review link (Google Business Profile short link)
- Ask patients after appointments (in person, the single most effective method)
- Send follow-up SMS/WhatsApp 24h after appointment with direct review link
- Respond to every review within 48 hours (positive and negative)
- Display Google reviews prominently on website (already have the component)

### Priority 4: Emergency/Urgency Landing Page
**Current:** Position 14-47 for urgency keywords (2,000+ combined impressions)
**Target:** Position 1-5

**Actions:**
- Create `/urgente` page in all 3 languages
- Target: "urgente stomatologice satu mare", "urgente dentare satu mare", "stomatologie urgenta satu mare", "stomatologie satu mare non-stop"
- Content: what counts as a dental emergency, what to do, hours, phone number, address
- Add click-to-call button and WhatsApp link
- Mark page as high-priority in sitemap

### Priority 5: Start a Blog (Content Marketing)
**Current:** Blog structure exists but no posts
**Target:** 2 posts/month, 12+ posts in first 6 months

**First 6 blog topics (targeting real search queries):**
1. "Cum alegi cel mai bun dentist in Satu Mare?" (targets "cel mai bun dentist satu mare" — 408 imp)
2. "Cat costa un implant dentar in Satu Mare? Ghid preturi 2026" (targets price keywords — 1,387+ imp)
3. "Aparat dentar in Satu Mare: tipuri, preturi si ce sa astepti" (targets "aparat dentar satu mare" — 1,063 imp)
4. "Urgente stomatologice: ce faci cand te doare masea noaptea?" (targets urgency keywords)
5. "Stomatologie copii: cand sa mergi prima data cu copilul la dentist" (supports children's dentistry page)
6. "Albire dentara profesionala vs. kit-uri de acasa: ce functioneaza" (targets whitening searches)
