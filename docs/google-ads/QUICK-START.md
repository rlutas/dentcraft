## ✅ Conversion Tracking (Tools → Conversions)

Import 3 conversii din GA4:

| Nume | GA4 event | Categorie | Primary? |
|---|---|---|---|
| Form Submission | `generate_lead` | Submit lead form | ✅ DA |
| Phone Click | `click_phone` | Phone calls | ❌ Secondary |
| WhatsApp Click | `click_whatsapp` | Other (Lead) | ❌ Secondary |

⚠️ Marchează `generate_lead` ca **Primary** — asta optimizează AI-ul.
